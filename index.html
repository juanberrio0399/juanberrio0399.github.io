01 — Profile

Engineer who ships, not just reports

I'm a Cloud & Data Engineer with 6+ years of experience. I don't just report the past — I design the cloud platforms that produce and serve the data.

My current project incrementally ingests an international courier's Excel files, consolidates them into a DuckDB warehouse, and publishes a serverless dashboard (Cloudflare + React + DuckDB-WASM) that updates itself three times a day — with no server to maintain and ~$0 infrastructure.

I design with an architecture mindset: separating compute, storage and presentation, choosing serverless, and optimizing for cost. Currently advancing on AWS and Systems Engineering, on the path to Cloud Solutions Architect.
