01 — About

Engineer who ships, not just reports

Hi! I'm Juan, a Cloud & Data Engineer from Bogotá 🇨🇴 with 6+ years of experience. I don't just report the past — I design the cloud platforms that produce and serve the data.

My current project incrementally ingests an international courier's Excel files, consolidates them into a DuckDB warehouse, and publishes a serverless dashboard (Cloudflare + React + DuckDB-WASM) that updates itself three times a day — with no server to maintain and ~$0 infrastructure.

I'm currently leveling up on AWS and Systems Engineering, on the path to Cloud Solutions Architect.

  • ☁️Architecture mindset: compute / storage / presentation, decoupled
  • ⚡Serverless & cost-obsessed (~$0 infra)
  • 🔄Production automation that runs unattended
  • 🤖AI / LLM integration & prompt engineering
